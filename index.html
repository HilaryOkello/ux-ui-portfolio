Keeping Athletes Hydrated Case Study

UX Research Personas User Journey Ideation Problem Statement Prototyping

This UX research aimed to improve runners' hydration through non-digital solutions. I analyzed user interviews and research on athletic hydration, and created a persona with a user journey map to pinpoint hydration challenges. The core problem identified was how to help runners measure and prepare their hydration. An ideation session generated ideas, and a storyboard visualized a potential solution. Key learnings included the importance of user-centered research in identifying solutions and the effectiveness of constraints in driving creative ideation.
